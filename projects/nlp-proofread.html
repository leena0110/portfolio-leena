Observations & Results

Key Findings:

  • The Vennify T5 model effectively corrected grammatical tense, subject-verb agreement, and basic spelling errors
  • Complex semantic inconsistencies remained in some cases (e.g., word choice changes slightly altering meaning)
  • Both models showed strong generalization without fine-tuning, suitable for small-scale NLP proofreading systems
  • The pipeline demonstrates practical application of transformer models for real-world text correction tasks
  • Error Ratio of 30% indicates room for improvement in handling complex grammatical structures
  • Diff Meaning Ratio of 58% suggests the model sometimes prioritizes grammatical correctness over meaning preservation
